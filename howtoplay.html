HOW TO PLAY

  • Select New Game

  • Choose the Difficulty level, Size of the Board and the Starting Player

  • Start the game whenever you're Ready!

  • All our NXN Boards work just like a regular 3X3 Tic Tac Toe Game.

  • To Win, you'll have to place your Human's Face in all the boxes of a Row, Column or a Diagonal.
